# Editorial Minimalist Design System (Winter 2026 Premium)

## Visual Philosophy
A high-end, editorial-inspired interface that prioritizes typography, negative space, and fluid motion. It feels like a premium lifestyle app rather than a utility.

## Core Aesthetics
- **Backgrounds**: Soft, warm "Paper" textures (`#FDFBF7`) and subtle gradients.
- **Typography**:
  - **Headings**: `Instrument Serif` (Italic for emphasis) - Large, elegant, and editorial.
  - **Body/UI**: `Geist` or `Inter` - Clean, high-readability sans-serif.
- **Colors**:
  - **Primary**: Deep "Ink" Black (`#1A1A19`).
  - **Accent**: Soft "Ocean" Blue (`#0066FF`) or "Tomato" Red (`#E60000`) used sparingly.
  - **Muted**: "Dust" Grey (`#908879`) for secondary info.
- **Interactivity**:
  - **Glassmorphism**: Blurred backgrounds for overlays and drawers.
  - **Micro-interactions**: Subtle scale-ups on hover, smooth spring transitions.
  - **Borders**: Hairline borders (`0.5px` or `1px`) with low opacity.

## Component Specs

### 1. Menu Item Card
- **Layout**: Clean vertical stack with a high-aspect-ratio image.
- **Typography**: Serif title, sans-serif price.
- **Interaction**: Image zooms slightly on hover; card has a soft shadow lift.

### 2. Payment Slider (Premium)
- **Track**: A slim, elegant track with a glass-like finish.
- **Handle**: A polished, metallic or soft-gradient circular handle.
- **Motion**: Fluid, elastic resistance as you slide.

### 3. Navigation / Bento
- **Bento**: Not rigid; use varying padding and soft-edged cards to create a rhythmic layout.
